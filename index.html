Omni Layer

An open-source, fully-decentralized asset platform on the Bitcoin Blockchain. Built on top of the Bitcoin blockchain. Omni is a platform for creating and trading custom digital assets and currencies. It is a software layer built on top of the most popular, most audited, most secure blockchain - Bitcoin. Omni transactions are Bitcoin transactions that enable next-generation features on the Bitcoin Blockchain.
